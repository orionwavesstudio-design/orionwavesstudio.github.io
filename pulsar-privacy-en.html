Privacy Policy — Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

PULSAR ("the App") is developed by OrionWavesStudio. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We are committed to protecting your privacy and comply with GDPR and applicable privacy laws.

2. Data We Collect

Anonymous identifier: The App uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication to assign a unique anonymous ID (UID) to each user. This ID is not linked to any personal information such as name, email, or phone number.

Game data: Your scores, combo history, AI archetype, Mastery stage, and game session data are stored locally on your device (Room database + DataStore) and optionally synchronized to Firebase Firestore for the global leaderboard.

Device information: Standard analytics data (device model, OS version, app version) may be collected via Firebase Analytics to improve performance.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We never collect: your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personally identifiable information. No account creation is required to use PULSAR.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your anonymous UID and game data are used solely to: display your scores on the global leaderboard, restore your progress if you reinstall the app, and power the AI difficulty engine that adapts to your playing style.

5. Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google LLC): Used for anonymous authentication, Firestore database, and Analytics. Firebase's privacy policy is available at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Google Play Billing: Used to process the one-time Premium purchase (€2.99). Payment data is handled entirely by Google and is never accessible to OrionWavesStudio.

6. Data Retention

Your anonymous game data is retained as long as your anonymous Firebase account exists. You can request deletion by contacting us (see Section 9). Uninstalling the app permanently removes all local data.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Since all data is anonymous, we cannot identify you personally — but we can delete all data associated with your anonymous UID upon request.

8. Children's Privacy

PULSAR is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data deletion requests:

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
